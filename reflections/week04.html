Week 04 Zen Garden Reflection

1. What was the most impactful thing you learned this week and why do you think so?

The most impactful thing I learned was how to use @use and @include in SCSS. By having different pieces of the CSS separated by files, this makes it easier to find things easier afterwards if anything needs to be changed. When I learned last week that I could have variables in CSS it made my heart happy. However, I was even happier when I learned this week you can have a whole file dedicated to just variables and call it in. This level of organization may take time to put together, but it is very much worth the effort. In the future, much time can be saved.
